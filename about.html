Our Mission

Our Mission & Vision

To make mathematics accessible, engaging, and enjoyable for every African learner, regardless of their background or location.

We believe that mathematical literacy is a fundamental right and a critical skill for Africa's development. Our vision is to create a continent where every student has the mathematical foundation needed to pursue STEM careers and contribute to technological innovation.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

School of Mathematics Nigeria was founded in 2018 by a team of passionate educators and mathematicians who recognized the systemic challenges facing mathematics education across Africa.

What began as a small tutoring initiative in Lagos has grown into a comprehensive educational ecosystem serving students, teachers, and parents across Nigeria and beyond.

Our journey has been guided by a simple but powerful belief: every student can excel in mathematics when given the right tools, support, and encouragement.

2018

Founded with just 20 students in a community center in Lagos

2019

Launched our digital platform, reaching 5,000 students online

2020

Pivoted to remote learning during pandemic, expanded to 3 countries

2021

Launched UThrive initiative for underserved communities

2022

Reached 100,000 learners milestone

2023

Expanded to 5 countries, launched Currigami curriculum studio
